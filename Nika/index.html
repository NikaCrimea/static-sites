SELECTORS OF GOOD THINGS

The human factor

We think diversity is a value that should be defended at all costs. We don't care about the colour of your skin or where you come from, all we want are happy, motivated and enthusiastic people. This is our team: unconventional people with loads of great qualities and talent.

Good things are for all

We select only the very best Sicilian ingredients, like Ragusano Buffalo meat and Sicilian beef, but also Madonie Provola cheese, Girgentana goat's cheese and lots of other yummylicious things. Tasty ingredients to savour one morsel at a time.

Respect for the earth

We believe in respect for the region in which we live and we also believe in the people who are dedicated to safeguarding our beautiful Sicily, a land rich in intense and genuine flavours that still has so much to give.
